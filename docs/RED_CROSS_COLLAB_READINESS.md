# Red Cross collaboration readiness plan

## What is already implemented

- Emergency-first UX: call and location sharing are always prioritized.
- Training-only AI language: avoids claiming certification.
- Local Medical ID and SOS sharing flow.
- Animated emergency guides for all situations.
- Video-ready content pipeline.
- Camera-based AI practice coach with on-device pose detection.

## What must be validated before launch

1. Emergency numbers and wording.
2. All first-aid steps and “avoid” warnings.
3. Animation accuracy.
4. Video scripts, subtitles, and voiceover.
5. AI posture metrics with instructor-labeled sessions.
6. Privacy, consent, and liability language.
7. Accessibility: Arabic, English, French, high-contrast, voice-first usage.

## AI Coach validation idea

Collect consented practice sessions with CPR manikins and certified instructors. Label:

- correct / incorrect hand placement
- elbow angle
- shoulder-over-hands alignment
- compression rhythm
- release consistency
- visibility failures

Use these labels to calibrate score thresholds and produce instructor-approved feedback.

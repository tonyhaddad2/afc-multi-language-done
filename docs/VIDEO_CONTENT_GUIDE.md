# Animation/video content guide

V5 replaces the small placeholder animations with 3D-style human mannequin MP4 modules for every supported emergency:

- CPR
- Choking
- Severe bleeding
- Burns
- Stroke
- Seizure
- Fire/smoke
- Car accident
- Electric shock
- Breathing problem
- Unconscious/recovery position
- Panic/distress
- Severe allergy
- Poisoning/overdose
- Explosion/collapse
- Drowning

The files are stored in `app/src/main/res/raw/` and are used by the guide video cards and the full-screen animation lesson.

For a real Red Cross collaboration, replace these prototype 3D-style animations with medically reviewed professional 3D animations or certified Red Cross video assets.

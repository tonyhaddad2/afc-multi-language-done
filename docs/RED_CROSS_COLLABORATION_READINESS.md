# Red Cross collaboration readiness plan

Implemented now:

- Fast SOS location sharing
- Lebanon emergency-number quick dial
- Animated guides and attached animated MP4 modules for every supported situation
- CPR metronome
- AI posture coach prototype
- Local Medical ID
- Trusted contact alert workflow

Important next milestone:

- The project should be developed toward V7 Connected Emergency MVP with secure backend integration, encrypted Medical ID, trusted-contact emergency access, and protected live-location sharing.

Must be validated before public health claims:

1. Emergency numbers and regional routing.
2. Every guide step and warning.
3. Every animation storyboard.
4. Every AI scoring rule and wording.
5. Camera coach false-positive and false-negative behavior.
6. Privacy and data handling.
7. Arabic, English, and French wording.
8. Accessibility under stress, low vision, and noisy conditions.
9. Security architecture for encrypted medical, contact, and location data.
10. Backend authorization for emergency access and emergency snapshots.

Recommended validation study:

- Record instructor-approved CPR practice clips on mannequins.
- Label landmark visibility, elbow angle, shoulder stack, rhythm, and hand position.
- Compare AI scores to instructor ratings.
- Define thresholds for feedback wording.
- Validate secure storage, transmission, and decryption rules before any public launch.
- Only after validation should the app say “approved” or “certified.”

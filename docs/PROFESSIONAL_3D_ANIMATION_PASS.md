## Important production note

These files are app-ready MP4 animations generated for the prototype/package. A true final commercial production pass would still require manual Blender/Maya/Cinema4D work, rig review, instructor review, and export from a dedicated 3D pipeline. This build gives the app a professional mannequin-style direction and removes the previous unusable placeholder feeling.

## AI Coach rule

The AI Coach remains camera-only:

- camera preview
- skeleton overlay
- auto-lock rescuer
- score/feedback

The guide/lesson screens play the 3D animation clips. The animations are not placed over the AI Coach camera.

# Electric storyboard

## Visual style
- clay/mannequin humans
- clean cyan medical floor
- no gore
- clear hand/body positioning
- soft studio lighting

## Camera
- 3/4 medical-training view
- full rescuer body visible when motion matters
- patient/mannequin visible only as needed
- avoid clutter

## Timeline
1. Establish safe scene and body position.
2. Show exact hand/body placement.
3. Show primary motion loop.
4. End on clear final position.
